"To-Plan"


 refers to a list of all tasks to be done. This is a list to visualize what you do so that you can easily see, prioritize your work, and make time management easier. "To-Plan" is a list of things to do. It is not easy to remember all these things only in your head. Moreover, in the case of a certification test schedule with a fixed period, accidents may occur that miss that period.


 The first step in creating a to-do list that does not fail is to divide the importance of work. Let's list what we have to do and list them in order of importance. What comes to the top? Making a checklist of what to do according to the order of importance is the first step in creating a to-do list.


 If the importance of work is also organized, the following is to make a weekly list according to the daily schedule. At this time, it is easy to identify what to do if classified in urgent order. For example, it is classified into three categories: the most urgent, the intermediate urgent, and the non-urgent. And when you're done, make the next plan by marking the cancellation. If you write down a week's list in the order of importance and urgency of what you have to do, you may not miss what you have to do.
